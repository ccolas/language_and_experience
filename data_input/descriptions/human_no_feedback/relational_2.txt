Use the arrow keys to move. Do not touch the white boxes it cost a life. The blue boxes have to be move into the yellow squares. To open up the yellow squares move the orange boxes into the red and other color ones. Then move the blue to the yellow it opens. Try to do in least amount of moves. White will kill you and so will running out of moves. Blue completes the yellow and orange opens up the colors into the yellow. Touching the pink on last level turns it into orange that you can then move into a color.
